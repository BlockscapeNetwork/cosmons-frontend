  14. Click on "Battle"
  15. Choose attacker monster address "monster112awrcsx" or your Monster/NFT ID
  16. Choose defender monster address "monster112sarcsb"
  17. Click "Battle !!!" (follow the steps in Keplr pop-up window)
  18. If you dont like your Monster anymore you can sell it on Marketplace
  19. If nobody want to buy your offered Monster you can withdraw it back
